When office hours end, patient intent does not.

This is Serenity Royale Hospital AI, created by ODIADEV AI.

A patient in Abuja searches for rehabilitation support and lands on the Serenity website. The WhatsApp button is clear, familiar, and available at the moment of need.

The patient opens WhatsApp. Dr Ade responds calmly, explains the next step, and guides the patient through a short booking flow: name, location, preferred doctor, center, date, and time.

The appointment request is captured after hours instead of being lost.

Then the system routes the right alerts. The patient gets confirmation. Abdullahi, the secretary, receives the action message for dashboard follow-up. Dr. K receives the oversight alert. And if the patient selected Dr. Grace, she receives the appointment summary too.

Inside the dashboard, the team can see pending AI bookings, message delivery, calendar review, recent AI activity, and system readiness.

If no doctor was selected, staff can assign one, confirm the appointment, retry alerts, and keep the patient informed without touching the database.

For crisis messages, the AI does not pretend to be a doctor. It responds safely, gives urgent contact guidance, and escalates to the care team.

Serenity AI keeps the hospital front door open: supporting patients, protecting after-hours demand, and aligning the people responsible for care.
